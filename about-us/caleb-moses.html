Caleb is the current primary contributor to Data Stories. He is a proud mathematician, currently employed as an analyst with a New Zealand Government ministry. His technical expertise spans data science, artificial intelligence, natural language processing and data visualisation.

His hobbies include programming, politics, board games, learning foreign languages, and desultory night time philosophy. He spends a lot of time on week-nights tinkering with statistical models, but likes to spend the weekend with his family and his girlfriend.

The views he expresses on this site are his own, and not those of his employers or especially the New Zealand government. He may occasionally blog about issues that crop up at work, but anything controversial that relates to work will be removed if and when it is found.
